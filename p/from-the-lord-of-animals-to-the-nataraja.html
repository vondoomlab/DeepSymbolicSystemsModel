From the Lord of Animals to the Nataraja

A traceable symbolic continuity from Mehrgarh (c. 7000 BCE) to the Nataraja bronze at CERN — nine thousand years in which a single symbolic complex encoding wildness, sovereignty, and cosmic generation survived civilisational collapse, migration, religious conquest, and scientific revolution.
